New Hampshire Conservation Lands
A summary of my summer fellowship with The Nature Conservancy for summer 2021.
Project Details
I'm grateful to have been a part of the University of New Hampshire's Sustainability Fellows summer program, in which I worked with The Nature Conservancy of New Hampshire as their GIS fellow. This project consisted of working with the state of New Hampshire's data repository, GRANIT, in updating the public and conservations lands dataset.
