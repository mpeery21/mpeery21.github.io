New Hampshire Conservation Lands

During summer 2021 I was lucky enough to spend the summer as a GIS Fellow with The Nature Conservancy in New Hampshire. My role consisted of updating the state-wide database of public and conservation lands, primarily by georeferencing and digitizing maps of newly conserved lands. I ended up adding over 30,000 new acres of land into the database! This map, which I made at the end of the summer as a culmination of my work, shows all the different types of protected land throughout the state.
